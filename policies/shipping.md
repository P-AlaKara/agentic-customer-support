# Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Processing Time
Orders are processed within 2 business days.
Processing may take longer during holidays or promotions.

## Delivery Time
Standard shipping usually takes 3â€“5 business days after dispatch.
Delivery estimates are not guarantees.

## Tracking
Customers receive a tracking link once the order ships.
If tracking is unavailable, escalate to support.

## Address Changes
Addresses cannot be changed after the order is shipped.
If not yet shipped, the agent may update the address.

## Lost Packages
If tracking shows delivered but the customer cannot find the package,
advise them to check with neighbors or building management.
If still missing after 48 hours, escalate.

## Delayed Shipments
If an order has not shipped after 5 business days, escalate.

## Split Shipments
Orders with multiple items may arrive in separate packages.

## International Shipping
Customs fees or import duties are the responsibility of the customer.

## Cancellation
Orders can be cancelled only before shipment.
If already shipped, the customer must follow the return process.

## Escalation Rules
Escalate if:
- carrier confirms loss,
- delivery exceeds 10 business days,
- customer reports repeated delivery failures.
